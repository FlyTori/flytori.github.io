Design Process

Design Workflow

To design for million-level user base products, we need to take consideration of accessibility, localization, copy and tone, etc. Based on the scope of the product, sometimes it is necessary to collaborate with different teams to review the design. We conducted a survey with internal developers and designers to identify some best practices and made a design workflow proposal.

  • Company: Mozilla
  • Time: 2016
  • Skills: Journey Map, Questionnaire, Workshop, Brainstorming

Design System

Design System has become popular these days. From huge companies like Microsoft, Google, to middle-sized Airbnb, Salesforce all have their design own system/language. Mozilla has product on different platforms on Windows, Mac, Linux, Android, and iOS. To deliver a more consistent visual style and facilitate the design process, we developed our own design system.

  • Company: Mozilla
  • Time: 2016-2017
  • Skills: Competitor Analysis, Ideation, Workshop
