Design Process, System, and Talks

Design Process

Designing for million user based products is exciting and challenging. Locationalization, accessibility, different culture and user behavior, collaboration with teams across the world - lots to consider and be taken care of. This is an study of internal study to understand and improve our end to end design process. We did a series of case study and proposed a more unified and efficient design process framework.


  • Company: Mozilla
  • Project Type: Internal Design Project
  • Time: Sep 2016 - Dec 2016
  • Role: UX designer
  • Team: 2 UX designer
