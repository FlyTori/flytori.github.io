Firefox Lite

Background

Firefox Rocket(now Firefox Lite) is a lightweight web browser customized for emerging markets. It reached 200K downloads with a rating of 4.5 within 2 months, and reached 1M downloads one year after launch in Indonesia.


I was one of the two main UX designers leading the project. I participated from early concept ideation, field research, prototyping, user testing to the official product launch.

  • Company: Mozilla
  • Time: March 2017 - December 2017
  • Role: UX designer
  • Team: 2 UX designer, 1 UX researcher, 1 Visual designer
  • Responsibilities: Competitor Analysis, Interaction Design, Usability Testing, UX spec, Research
