Early Projects

Click Phone

In 2013, the market share of feature phones was between 30% to 40%. At that time, there was a strong need for smart feature phones for elders. Elders want to connect with other family members, friends and the world via internet with feature phone - the interaction and form factor they were familiar with. This was a conceptual design of Firefox OS UI customized for elders.


  • Company: Mozilla
  • Product Type: Feature Phone
  • Time: March 2015 - July 2015
  • Role: UX designer
  • Team: 2 UX designer, 1 Visual designer
  • Responsibilities: Concept Design, Interaction Design, Mockup, Market Research, Prototyping
