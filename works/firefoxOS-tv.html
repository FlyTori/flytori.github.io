Firefox OS Smart TV

Firefox OS powers Panasonic's 2016 & 2017 UHD TVs.

Smart TV Home Screen & Navigation Model

Designing for TV is quite different from designing for Apps or websites. When lying on the couch 10 feet away from the TV, people are relaxed and can only process one piece of information at one time. The most distinguishable UI of Firefox OS TV is the horizontal one row UI and "Pin" feature. For general users, they can find what they want with minimal effort. For advanced users, they can customize their home-screen and move their apps based on frequency.

TV Browser

Do people use the browser on TV? Actually, some people really do! A lot of web videos are not accessible through Youtube, Netflix, and Hulu. Additionally, many countries don't have these services yet. Many people use tv browser to watch cartoons, sports games, the latest tv shows, etc. Based on our studies, we enhanced the video experience by supporting more formats of videos, making it possible to pause and resume web videos via traditional remote control. In addition to that, we also designed the D-pad mode for TV browser. It allows users to highlight the links on the webpages by using the up, down, right, and left keys.

  • Company: Mozilla
  • Time: 2015
  • Skills: UX Spec, Mockups

Remote Control App

TV remote control is an essential design challenge for all designers. In early days, there were no remote control apps or touchpads on a remote control, thus resulting in big remote controls with more than 30 keys. This app, designed for Firefox OS TV, aims to replace the traditional physical remote control when it comes to typing or watching web videos in TV browser. The main screen is a big touchpad so people can use it even while they are watching tv. We spent some time tuning the acceleration rate and performance of the touchpad to make sure it works well on different screen sizes.

  • Company: Mozilla
  • Time: 2015
  • Skills: UX Spec, Prototyping, Mockups, Competitor Analysis, Brainstorming Workshop
